WELCOME TO RAINBOW SIX SIEGE

Rainbow Six Siege is an online tactical shooter developed by Ubisoft. Engage in intense 5v5 battles, employing strategy, teamwork, and a wide variety of operators each with unique abilities. Whether you're breaching walls, defusing bombs, or rescuing hostages, every decision and shot counts. Dive into the highly destructible environments and experience unparalleled tactical realism.

ICONIC OPERATORS

SLEDGE

Sledge, the powerhouse of Rainbow Six Siege, wields his signature tactical breaching hammer to obliterate walls and barriers with bone-crushing force.

ASH

Ash, the swift and relentless demolitions expert of Rainbow Six Siege, uses her explosive breaching rounds to blast through walls and obstacles with lightning speed.

JÄGER

Jäger, the defensive mastermind of Rainbow Six Siege, deploys his sophisticated Active Defense System to intercept and neutralize incoming projectiles.

Despite the barbed wire and reinforced walls that Rainbow Six Siege makes you walk through in order to get to the meat of the gameplay, I can’t stop thinking about how much fun that gameplay is. I’ve already made new online friends with like-minded players who embrace Siege’s team-centric mechanics. It’s more cerebral than the twitch-and-react shooters of the day like Call of Duty and Halo – which doesn’t make Siege better or worse, but it does make it refreshingly different.
